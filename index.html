About Maa Gayatri Solar Solutions

Maa Gayatri Solar Solutions (MGSS) is a premier provider of sustainable energy solutions. Since our inception, we have been dedicated to illuminating homes and powering industries with clean, reliable, and cost-effective solar energy.

Our mission is to accelerate the transition to renewable energy by making solar accessible to everyone. We handle everything from consultation and design to installation and maintenance, ensuring a seamless experience for our clients. We believe in quality, integrity, and a brighter, greener future for generations to come.
